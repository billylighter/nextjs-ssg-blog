- **Q:** What is SSG?  
  **A:** Static Site Generation builds pages at **build time**.

- **Q:** Can I use Markdown?  
  **A:** Yes, Markdown files integrate easily with `remark` and `gray-matter`.

- **Q:** Can I have dynamic routes?  
  **A:** Use `getStaticPaths` for dynamic static pages.
